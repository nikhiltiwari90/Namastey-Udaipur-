## OKR Retrospective

### What drove overperformance (Q2)
- AIESEC network seeding solved cold start faster than expected
- Tamil traveler community responded strongly — organic word of mouth within travel groups
- Cold email channel outperformed expectations (~40% open rate)

### Root cause of Q4 misses
The single biggest miss: **in-app review submission was never shipped.** This was the highest-leverage feature for the content flywheel and community growth, and it was deprioritised due to engineering bandwidth. Every Q4 KR that missed can be traced back to this one decision.

**Lesson:** In a community product, the content contribution loop is not a nice-to-have feature. It IS the product's growth engine. It should never be cut from an MVP.
